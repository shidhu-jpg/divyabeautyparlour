Owner se Milen

Hello, main Chandani Gupta hoon, Divya Beauty Parlour ki owner.

Main beauty field mein pichhle 6+ saalon se kaam kar rahi hoon. Jo cheez ek simple interest se shuru hui, woh dheere-dheere regular practice, learning aur real clients ke saath kaam karte-karte ek profession ban gayi.

Main zyada focus bridal makeup par karti hoon, kyunki mujhe pata hai ki woh din kitna special hota hai. Mera focus heavy ya overdone looks par nahi hota, balki aise makeup par hota hai jo comfortable lage aur person ko suit kare. Mera maanna hai ki bride ko confident aur relaxed feel karna chahiye, na ki apne look ko lekar nervous.

Divya Beauty Parlour mein hygiene ko hum bahut seriously lete hain aur achhe quality ke products use karte hain. Isse bhi zyada important baat yeh hai ki hum clients ki baat dhyaan se sunte hain. Har client ki preference alag hoti hai, aur hum usse samajhne ke liye time lete hain.

Hum apni services ko affordable aur sabke liye accessible rakhne ki koshish karte hain.

Hamare 80% se zyada clients repeat customers hain, aur hum unke support ke liye dil se thankful hain – woh hamare family jaise hain.

Yeh parlour trust aur word-of-mouth par bana hai, aur main un sabhi logon ki grateful hoon jinhone saalon se hume support kiya. Main aapka welcome karne ka intezaar kar rahi hoon.

Hamari Story

Divya Beauty Parlour ek simple soch se shuru hua: yeh kaam aur behtar tarike se ho sakta hai.

Isliye maine khud yeh kaam seekhne ka faisla kiya. Time, practice aur experience ke saath, maine aisi services deni shuru ki jo comfort, cleanliness aur natural results par focus karti hain.

Dheere-dheere log mere kaam par trust karne lage. Aaj Divya Beauty Parlour Areraj mein bridal makeup, skincare aur regular beauty treatments ke liye jaana jaata hai.

Hum perfection ka wada nahi karte — hum honesty, care aur har client ke respect ka wada karte hain.

Meet the Owner

Hello, I'm Chandani Gupta, the owner of Divya Beauty Parlour.

I have been working in the beauty field for over 6+ years. What started as a simple interest slowly turned into a profession through regular practice, learning, and experience with real clients.

I mostly work with bridal makeup, because I understand how important that day is. My focus is not on heavy or overdone looks, but on makeup that feels comfortable and suits the person. I believe a bride should feel confident and relaxed, not nervous about how she looks(I know if you Know this if you meet a bride on her wedding day.).

At Divya Beauty Parlour, we take hygiene seriously and use good-quality products. More importantly, we listen. Every client has different preferences, and we take time to understand what they are looking for.

We Try To Make our services affordable and ascessible to all.

We have more than 80% of repeatable costumers and we are grateful for their support, they are like our family.

This parlour is built on trust and word-of-mouth, and I'm grateful to everyone who has supported us over the years. I look forward to welcoming you.

Our Story

Divya Beauty Parlour started because of a simple thought: this can be done better.

I once visited a parlour where hygiene was poor and prices were high, but the service felt careless. That experience made me realize how important trust and cleanliness are in beauty services.

So i decided to learn the work myelf. With time, practice, and experience, i began offering services that focused on comfort, cleanliness, and natural results.

Slowly, people started trusting my work. Today, Divya Beauty Parlour serves clients across Areraj for bridal makeup, skincare, and regular beauty treatments.
